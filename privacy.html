Quick Summary: We process your images to create horror transformations using AI services. We don't store your images permanently and only collect minimal data needed for the app to function.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Images: Photos you upload for horror transformation
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, features used, and user preferences
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Purchase Information: Subscription and payment data (processed by Apple App Store)
  • Crash Data: Technical information when the app crashes (anonymous)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide AI-powered image transformation services
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Process premium subscriptions and purchases
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Analyze app usage to improve features
  • Ensure app security and prevent abuse

3. Image Processing and AI Services

Important: Images you upload are processed by third-party AI services (OpenAI and Replicate) to create horror transformations. Your images are:

  • Sent securely to AI processing services
  • Processed to create transformations
  • Automatically deleted after processing
  • Not stored permanently by us or AI providers

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share data with the following trusted partners:

  • AI Service Providers: OpenAI and Replicate for image processing
  • Analytics Services: Anonymous usage statistics for app improvement
  • Payment Processors: Apple App Store for subscription management
  • Cloud Services: Secure cloud infrastructure for app functionality

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Regular security assessments

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain data for the following periods:

  • Images: Deleted immediately after processing
  • Usage Data: Up to 2 years for analytics
  • Account Data: Until you delete the app
  • Purchase Data: As required by Apple App Store

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Delete the app and all local data
  • Contact us about your data
  • Opt out of analytics (via device settings)
  • Manage subscription settings (via App Store)
  • Request information about your data

8. Children's Privacy

HorrorAI is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States, where our AI service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through:

  • App Store update notifications
  • In-app notifications
  • Email (if provided)

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@horrorai.app
  • Support: support@horrorai.app
